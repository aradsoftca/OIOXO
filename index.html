Questions

Good to know.

Does my code really stay on my device?

Yes. The AI model runs in your browser or app on your own hardware; the only thing we ever measure is the volume of AI generation (token counts). Your code and prompts are never uploaded — it's the architecture, not a toggle.

Is it actually free?

Forever free to start — no card, no trial clock. The free tier includes the complete IDE and an allowance that refills every few hours. Pro ($3.99/mo) simply removes the wait; model access stays free either way.

What's the first-build experience like?

The first time, OIOXO downloads the on-device coder once (then it's cached and works offline). After that, you describe an app and watch it plan, write, run, and repair the project live — on your machine.

Can I use my own API key or a frontier model?

Yes — bring your own key from 12+ providers or run a local Ollama model. BYOK is free for everyone, and OIOXO's context engine trims your token bill by roughly 90%.

What is the Compute Mesh?

Pair devices you own on the same Wi-Fi. The lighter device borrows the stronger one's compute to build faster — and the work it does lifts your free limit. It's peer-to-peer and serverless; nothing routes through us.
