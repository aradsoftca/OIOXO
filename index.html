Questions

Good to know.

Does my code really stay on my device?

Yes. The AI runs on your own computer or phone — not on our servers. The only thing we ever count is how much you've used (a number), never the code or questions themselves. They're never uploaded. It's not a setting you switch on; it's just how OIOXO is built.

Is it actually free?

Free to start — no card, no countdown. You get the whole thing plus an allowance that refills every few hours. Using the AI models is always free. If you want unlimited time, Pro is $3.99/month — but the models stay free either way.

How does it save me ~90% on AI?

AI tools charge by the word, and they read far more of your project than they need. OIOXO sends the AI only the small part that's actually relevant — same answer, a fraction of the words. It even works inside Copilot, Cursor and Claude, so your existing setup gets cheaper without switching tools.

How do my devices "build together"?

Put two of your own devices on the same Wi-Fi and tap "Add a device" — show a code on one, scan it on the other. Now they work on the same project, and the weaker one borrows the stronger one's power. The time they spend helping each other adds free build time to your account. They talk directly — nothing goes through us.

Can I use my own AI (like Claude or OpenAI)?

Yes — plug in your own key from 12+ providers, or run a model locally with Ollama. It's free for everyone, and OIOXO still trims the bill by about 90%.
